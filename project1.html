E-commerce Platform Optimization

In this project, I led the optimization efforts for an e-commerce platform, focusing on improving user experience and increasing conversion rates.

Problem Statement

The existing e-commerce platform was facing issues with high bounce rates and low conversion rates. Users were finding it difficult to navigate the site and complete purchases.

Solution

We conducted user research and A/B testing to identify key pain points and implement improvements. Here are some of the changes we made:

  • Streamlined checkout process
  • Improved search functionality
  • Enhanced mobile responsiveness

Results

The optimizations resulted in a 25% increase in conversion rates and a 30% decrease in bounce rates. These improvements significantly boosted the platform's performance and customer satisfaction.
